Page Design

Page 1: Home

  • Name of Page: Home
  • Purpose: To provide a landing screen with accessible links to the other pages, as well as an introduction to the site as a whole.
  • Audience/Users: Clients (current and potential) of CGCS.
  • Content of the Page: This page contains the navigation bar and a brief introduction to the company.

Page 2: About Us

  • Name of Page: About Us
  • Purpose: To provide information about the company, its backstory, and its founder.
  • Audience/Users: People who wish to learn more about the company and its background.
  • Content of the Page: An image of the founder with a brief introduction, followed by a short description of how the company came to be.

Page 3: Services

  • Name of Page: Services
  • Purpose: To provide information on the services offered by CGCS.
  • Audience/Users: People who wish to learn more about the services offered by CGCS.
  • Content of the Page: This page will contain a list of services offered by CGCS, and a short explanation for each of these.

Page 4: Gallery

  • Name of Page: Gallery
  • Purpose: To provide photographic examples of CGCS's past or current projects.
  • Audience/Users: People who wish for a visual representation or documentation of the work CGCS does.
  • Content of the Page: Mainly images. I want to design this in such a way that clicking on an image will enlarge it without leaving the gallery page. I'll have to think of some ways to do this.

Page 5: Contact Us

  • Name of Page: Contact Us
  • Purpose: To provide multiple methods for clients to contact the company.
  • Audience/Users: People who wish to contact the company.
  • Content of the Page: A link to the founder's LinkedIn page, a list of multiple methods of contact, and the company's physical mailing address. I'd also like a button that says "get a quote" that would allow users to send the company a message straight from the site.

Dynamic Functionality:

  • Gallery Interface: I wish to include a working photo gallery interface that allows users to enlarge and scroll through images without needing to link to an external site.
  • Contact Form: I wish to provide a form in the "contact us" page that will allow users to send messages to the company straight from the website, within the site.
